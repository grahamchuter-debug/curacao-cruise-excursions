Why Klein Curacao Is the
Ultimate Curacao Day Trip

Southeast of Willemstad lies Klein Curacao — an uninhabited island surrounded by powder-white sand, a historic lighthouse and the rusted hull of a shipwreck just offshore. Its remote Caribbean shores offer crystal turquoise water and an atmosphere of pure, unspoiled escape.

Klein Curacao day trips are designed for luxury cruise guests who want more than a routine port day. From pier-side boat pickup, every detail — transfer timing, beach time, snorkeling and return to ship — is handled with the precision discerning travellers expect.

Whether this is your first Caribbean island escape or your return to a beach you never forgot, Klein Curacao transforms a cruise stop into something genuinely extraordinary.

Sample Itinerary

Your Klein Curacao Day Trip

A cruise-friendly itinerary from Willemstad pier to a remote Caribbean island and back aboard, with time to spare before departure.

1
Pier pickup

Meet at Willemstad

Your guide meets you at Mega Pier, Mathey Wharf or a nearby marina within minutes of disembarkation. Confirm exact pickup when booking your Klein Curacao day trip.

2
Boat transfer

Cross to Klein Curacao

A scenic boat ride across open Caribbean water to the uninhabited island — typically 1.5 to 2 hours from Willemstad, depending on sea conditions.

3
Island time

Beach, lighthouse & snorkeling

Explore white sand beaches, visit the historic lighthouse, photograph the shipwreck and snorkel in crystal clear water — typically 3 to 4 hours on the island.

4
Return

Back to pier with buffer time

Boat return to Willemstad with ample time before your ship's all aboard call.
