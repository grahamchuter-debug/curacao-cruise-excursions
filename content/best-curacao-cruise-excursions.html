Find the Right Shore Excursion
For Your Curacao Port Day

Curacao offers some of the Caribbean's most memorable cruise port experiences, from Klein Curacao day trips and sea turtle snorkeling to catamaran sailing, beach excursions and colourful Willemstad walking tours. Use this guide to compare popular excursion types and top rated experiences before you book.

Many operators run small groups with flexible pickup near Mega Pier or Mathey Wharf, and plan returns with buffer time before your ship's all aboard call. USD is widely accepted alongside the local guilder (ANG).
